Common Questions about Pricing

How is the offer price for my home determined?

Your home is thoroughly evaluated by a team of experts who specialize in your market. With their technical know-how, along with Turbo Offers’ propriety evaluation and market analysis technologies, Turbo Offers has the ability to provide our customers with the most competitive offer available.

Are there other transaction costs that I should consider?

Certain costs are shouldered by the seller- similar to any traditional sale. These fees may include title insurance, escrow fees, HOA transfers fees, taxes, document transfers and other customary fees. These fees may vary based on the market and can range from 1% to 3% of purchase price. The Turbo Offers team will help you work with the title company or attorney’s office handling the transaction to get an estimate of your net proceeds.

How does my offer price compare to values on sites like Zillow?

The home values as seen on websites such as Zillow, Trulia, Realtor.com, and Redfin, are determined by the site’s individual computer-generated algorithms. These values are based on information available to them through public record therefore the information is limited. The data collected focused on the market’s history, not its future. Although our offer is not intended to be an appraisal in any form, it is a real and actionable offer. It is carefully based on the evaluation done by real estate experts. All of these factors in addition to the individual features and condition of your home will determine the home value in accordance to the market’s inventory and its future supply.
