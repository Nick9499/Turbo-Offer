How it works

The main concept behind Turbo Offers:

We’re all about easy.

One of the easiest ways to sell your home is direct to TurboOffers. We will always ask the seller to provide us with a few details about their home. When we do that we will give them a free, no-obligation offer on their house.

When selling to us this is what happens:

  • They get a competitive cash offer within 24 hours
  • No open houses: There will be no need for real estate open houses or interrupted weekends because of constant showings
  • Flexible closing date: They have the ability to pick their closing day and can get flexible move out options
  • Free moving: If they’re moving local, within a 60 mile radius, we will pay for it! This is for homes up to 2700 sq for and for 12,500 lbs. If the house is bigger they can call their case manager
  • Extended stay: they can avoid moving to a storage facility and then to the house. We can give them up to 3 days past the closing to move into their new home
  • If they like selling their house the old way with a realtor. We will save them the headache of finding the right realtor. We will be partnering up with top local agents in the market with the experience needed to follow the traditional sales process.

Other options we offer besides the free moving and realtor services:

  • If the client prefers to keep their house or fix the house before listing with a realtor. We can provide contractor services. That will include the following.
    • Painting
    • Plumbing
    • Carpentry
    • Masonry
    • Tiling
    • Flooring
    • Electrical
  • Ability to have access to the top lenders in the nation. We will supply top rated loan officers to help navigate the home buying process so they can sell their current property with ease and buy their following property with ease.
  • Refinancing services
  • Loan modification services
  • Stop auction consultations
  • Short sale services
